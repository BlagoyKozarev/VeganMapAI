Vegan Score Methodology

Our AI-powered scoring system evaluates restaurants across 6 key dimensions to provide transparent, reliable vegan-friendliness ratings.

Scoring Dimensions

🌱 Menu Variety
Number and quality of vegan options available
🏷️ Clear Labeling
How well vegan items are marked and identified
👨‍🍳 Staff Knowledge
Staff understanding of vegan requirements
🛡️ Cross-Contamination Prevention
Measures to avoid mixing with non-vegan items
📱 Online Information
Availability of vegan info on website/apps
🎨 Creativity & Innovation
Unique and creative plant-based offerings

Score Ranges

  • 8.0-10.0: Excellent vegan destination
  • 6.0-7.9: Good vegan options available
  • 4.0-5.9: Limited but acceptable choices
  • 2.0-3.9: Minimal vegan options
  • 0.0-1.9: Not recommended for vegans
