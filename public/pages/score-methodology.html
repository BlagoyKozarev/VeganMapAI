Vegan Score Methodology

Our AI-powered vegan scoring system analyzes multiple factors to provide a comprehensive assessment of how vegan-friendly a restaurant is. The score ranges from 0 to 10, with 10 being a fully vegan establishment.

8-10 Excellent
6-7.9 Good
4-5.9 Moderate
0-3.9 Limited

Score Components

1. Menu Purity

Weight: 30%

Evaluates the proportion and quality of vegan options on the menu.

  • 100% vegan menu: 10 points
  • Dedicated vegan section: 7-9 points
  • Multiple vegan options: 4-6 points
  • Few or no vegan options: 0-3 points

2. Clear Labeling

Weight: 20%

Assesses how clearly vegan options are marked and communicated.

  • Clear vegan symbols/labels: 8-10 points
  • Vegan items listed separately: 5-7 points
  • Must ask staff: 2-4 points
  • No clear identification: 0-1 points

3. Staff Knowledge

Weight: 15%

Measures staff understanding of vegan dietary requirements.

  • Expert knowledge: 8-10 points
  • Good understanding: 5-7 points
  • Basic knowledge: 2-4 points
  • Limited understanding: 0-1 points

4. Cross-Contamination Prevention

Weight: 15%

Evaluates measures to prevent cross-contamination with animal products.

  • Separate preparation areas: 8-10 points
  • Dedicated equipment: 5-7 points
  • Basic precautions: 2-4 points
  • No special measures: 0-1 points

5. Variety & Innovation

Weight: 10%

Considers the creativity and variety of vegan offerings.

  • Innovative vegan cuisine: 8-10 points
  • Good variety: 5-7 points
  • Basic options: 2-4 points
  • Very limited choices: 0-1 points

6. Price Accessibility

Weight: 10%

Assesses the affordability of vegan options compared to non-vegan items.

  • Same or lower price: 8-10 points
  • Slightly higher (+10-20%): 5-7 points
  • Moderately higher (+20-40%): 2-4 points
  • Significantly higher (+40%): 0-1 points

Calculation Formula

Final Score = Σ(Component Score × Component Weight) Example: Menu Purity: 8 × 0.30 = 2.4 Clear Labeling: 7 × 0.20 = 1.4 Staff Knowledge: 6 × 0.15 = 0.9 Cross-Contamination: 5 × 0.15 = 0.75 Variety: 7 × 0.10 = 0.7 Price: 8 × 0.10 = 0.8 Total Score = 6.95

Example: Fully Vegan Restaurant

A 100% vegan restaurant typically scores 9.0-10.0 because it excels in menu purity, has no cross-contamination concerns, and staff are fully knowledgeable about vegan requirements.

Data Sources

Our AI analyzes multiple data sources to calculate scores:

  • Restaurant menus and websites
  • Customer reviews and ratings
  • Social media mentions
  • Direct restaurant surveys
  • Community contributions

Score Updates

Scores are recalculated periodically as new data becomes available. Restaurants can request a manual review if they've made significant changes to their vegan offerings.
