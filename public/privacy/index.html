Privacy Policy

VeganMapAI respects your privacy and complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, EU 2016/679).

Data Controller

VeganMapAI. Contact: support@veganmapai.ai

Personal Data We Process

  • Name and email received from your chosen authentication provider (Google, Meta/Facebook, Twitter/X, Apple).
  • Technical logs required for security and fraud prevention (timestamps, IP, user agent).
  • Restaurant preferences, favorites, and vegan scores you provide voluntarily.

Purpose and Legal Basis

We use your data only for authentication, account management, personalized restaurant recommendations, and securing access to the platform. Legal basis: your explicit consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) and legitimate interests for security (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

Storage and Retention

Data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud, EU/US regions) and PostgreSQL database. We retain your data while your account exists or until you request deletion.

Sharing and Transfers

No sale of data. We share data only with the selected login provider you use to authenticate and with infrastructure vendors strictly for hosting and security. International transfers, where applicable, rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs).

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction and objection to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Lodging a complaint with your local Supervisory Authority.

Children

The service is not directed to children under the age required by local law for consent to online services.

Security

We apply industry-standard security and access controls. No authentication provider tokens are shared outside our systems.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Maps API for location services and Google Analytics for usage statistics. These services have their own privacy policies.

Contact and Requests

For any privacy request, email support@veganmapai.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

Updates

We may update this policy. Continued use means you accept the latest version.
